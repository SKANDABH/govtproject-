Government Departments

Medical Department

The Medical Department is responsible for healthcare and medical services.

Public Works Department (PWD)

The PWD is responsible for infrastructure development and maintenance.

Education Department

The Education Department oversees public education and schools.

Finance Department

The Finance Department manages government finances and budgeting.

Transportation Department

The Transportation Department handles public transportation and road networks.

Environmental Protection Agency (EPA)

The EPA is responsible for environmental conservation and regulations.

Department of Justice

The Department of Justice oversees legal matters and law enforcement.

Department of Agriculture

The Department of Agriculture manages agricultural policies and practices.

Department of Labor

The Department of Labor focuses on labor laws, employment, and workers' rights.
